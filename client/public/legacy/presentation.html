8 Datastores

Far more than a database - a composed stack enabling search, queries, relationships, permissions, and temporal analysis

01
Context Registry
The map legend for AI
Stores annotations describing the purpose and usage of structured data elements. Transforms static schemas into actionable business intelligence that AI agents can reason over.

Data Warehouse
Large-scale analytics
Stores large, structured datasets optimized for economic, large-scale analytics. Powers NL2SQL queries, reporting dashboards, and batch aggregations across the full dataset.
Click to expand
03
Relational Database
Transactional data
Stores highly structured, transactional data with ACID guarantees. The system of record for object state, field values, and real-time reads/writes.
Click to expand
04
Inverted Keyword Index
Syntactic search
Powers keyword and syntactic search across all objects. Enables exact-match lookups, field-specific filtering, and traditional full-text search capabilities.
Click to expand
05
Vector Database
Semantic search
Stores vector embeddings for meaning-based search. Surfaces semantically similar objects even when keywords don't match - finds the "intent" behind queries.
Click to expand
06
Graph Database
Relationships & documents
Stores relationships and extensible connections between data entities, as well as documents and files. The connective tissue that maps how everything relates.
Click to expand
07
Permissions Store
Access control
Maintains permission mapping of every graph entity, field, and user. Ensures AI answers respect who is asking - data leakage is architecturally impossible.
Click to expand
08
Time Series Database
Temporal analysis
Stores time-indexed data for temporal analysis and "time travel". Enables questions like "what changed last week" and complete change history across all objects.
